HTML Headings

HTML headings are used to define titles or subtitles on a webpage. There are six levels of headings from <h1> to <h6>.

Examples of Headings:

  • <h1> – Biggest heading
  • <h2> – Second level heading
  • <h3> – Third level heading
  • <h4> – Fourth level heading
  • <h5> – Fifth level heading
  • <h6> – Smallest heading

Actual Output:

<h1> Heading 1 </h1>

<h2> Heading 2 </h2>

<h3> Heading 3 </h3>

<h4> Heading 4 </h4>

<h5> Heading 5 </h5>
<h6> Heading 6 </h6>
